Jiffy Cornbread Casserole

Ingredients

  • 1 can whole corn (17 oz)
  • 1 can creamed corn (17 oz)
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • ½ cup margarine, melted
  • 1 package Jiffy Corn Muffin mix (8½ oz)
  • 2 eggs

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 300°F.
  2. Drain can of whole corn (not creamed corn).
  3. Combine all ingredients into a large bowl and mix thoroughly.
  4. Pour into a 2-quart casserole dish.
  5. Bake for 1 hour and 40 minutes.
